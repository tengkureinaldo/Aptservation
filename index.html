Frequently Asked Questions

These are the frequently asked questions about Aptservation.

1. What is Aptservation??

Aptservation is an app that allows you to list your apartment for rent or book apartments for short or long term stays — all in one platform.

2. How do I list my apartment on Aptservation??

Simply sign up, add your apartment details and photos, then choose registered property agent in our app. Once approved, your listing will be live!

3. How can I book an apartment on Aptservation?

Simply sign up, choose your apartment, and make a booking request. Once approved, you can complete your payment — and voilà! You're good to go.

4. How do I pay for a booking on Aptservation?

You can pay securely through the app using major credit cards or supported digital wallets. All payments are encrypted and processed through trusted payment gateways.

5. Who should I contact for more information about an apartment?

You can contact the property agent directly using the contact details provided in each listing. If you need further help, feel free to reach out to Aptservation support.
